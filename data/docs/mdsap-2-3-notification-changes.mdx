<Subtask actionitem={"Interview top management about changes as input to sampling."}>
Top management should have awareness of change and facilitate efficient identification changes relevant to sampling. 
</Subtask>

<Subtask actionitem={"Review change and notification procedures"}>
Change procedures commonly are the same or extend the procedures reviewed in <QLink>MDSAP 1-8 Document and record control</QLink>. Ensure they include instruction relevant to the markets identified in <QLink>MDSAP 2-2 Evidence of marketing clearance or approval</QLink>. 
</Subtask>

<Subtask actionitem={"Review established change logs and sample changes"}>
The changes described by management can be confirmed against the established change logs relevant to document and change control. Confirm no other relevant changes exist or potentially include them in sampling.
</Subtask>
